LIST OF MOVIES


1. AVATAR

Summary: In the future, paraplegic marine Jake Sully goes to Pandora, a moon inhabited by the Na'vi, a race of blue-skinned humanoids. Jake becomes an avatar, a human-Na'vi hybrid, and infiltrates the Na'vi clans in order to gain their trust and convince them to leave their home so that humans can mine for unobtanium, a valuable mineral. However, Jake falls in love with Neytiri, a Na'vi woman, and comes to see the Na'vi way of life as something worth protecting. He ultimately fights alongside the Na'vi against the humans in a battle for Pandora.

2. AVENGERS ENDGAME

Summary: After Thanos wipes out half of all sentient life in the universe, the remaining Avengers must work together to find a way to reverse the damage and bring back those who were lost. The film is the culmination of the Infinity Saga, which began with Iron Man in 2008.

3. AVATAR THE WAY OF WATER

Summary: A decade after the events of the first film, Jake Sully lives peacefully with Neytiri and their children on Pandora. However, their tranquility is shattered when the "Sky People" return to extract the remaining unobtanium. Jake must once again gather the Na'vi clans and protect their home.

4. Star Wars: Episode IV - A New Hope (1977)

Summary: In a galaxy far, far away, a group of rebels fight against the evil Galactic Empire. Princess Leia is captured by Darth Vader, the Empire's enforcer. Luke Skywalker, a young farm boy, joins forces with smuggler Han Solo and his Wookiee copilot Chewbacca to rescue the princess. Together, they become heroes of the Rebel Alliance.

5. Titanic (1997)

Summary: the doomed RMS Titanic in 1912, a young and penniless Jack Dawson and first-class passenger Rose DeWitt Bukater fall in love. However, their love is challenged by their different social classes and the impending disaster of the ship's sinking.

6. Jurassic Park (1993)

Summary: In Isla Nublar, a wealthy entrepreneur creates a theme park featuring genetically recreated dinosaurs. However, the park's security systems fail, and the dinosaurs go on a rampage. A paleontologist, a lawyer, and two grandchildren must find a way to escape the island.

7. The Lion King (1994)

Summary: Simba is a young lion cub who is destined to become king. However, his evil uncle Scar murders Simba's father, Mufasa, and takes over the pride. Simba goes into exile, but years later he returns to challenge Scar and take his rightful place as king.
